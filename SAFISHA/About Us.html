About Us

Safisha is a leading platform...

Safisha is a leading platform that provides comprehensive waste management solutions, connecting households to collectors and collectors to recyclers. It serves as a vital link in the chain of environmental sustainability, offering a seamless and efficient waste disposal system for residents and businesses alike. With a strong emphasis on promoting a cleaner and greener future, it has become a trusted destination for numerous households and waste collectors across the country.
