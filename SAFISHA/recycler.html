Recycler

DID YOU KNOW

companies like PETCO are involved in recycling plastics. PETCO, in particular, focuses on the recycling of polyethylene terephthalate (PET) plastics, commonly used in beverage bottles, food packaging, and other consumer products. They operate recycling programs to collect, sort, and process PET plastics, contributing to waste reduction and environmental sustainability efforts. Recycling plastics helps to conserve resources, reduce landfill waste, and minimize environmental pollution.
